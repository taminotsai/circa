What are the criteria for a language to be taken into account in CIRCABC?
 

You will find there further information on the languages which can be used in CIRCABC.

At the user interface level

The User Interface is being translated to the 23 official languages of the European Union.

These official languages are ordered alphabetically. They are coded according to the ISO "codes for the representation of names of languages" --Part 1: Alpha 2 codes (ISO639-1, alpha-2).

At the content level

CIRCABC deals with 165 languages defined and listed in ISO 639-1, alpha-2.

These languages are ordered as follows:

  • The official languages of the European Union appear at the top of the languages list;
  • The official languages of the candidate countries come in a second position;
  • They are followed by all other languages defined in the ISO 639-1 standard.
