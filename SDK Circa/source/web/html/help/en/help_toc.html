CIRCABC online help: Table of contents
 

Welcome to the online version of the CIRCABC user guide.

These pages aim to help you in your daily use of CIRCABC. They intend for first-time and advanced users.They are not aimed for the Interest Group leaders. If you are about to head an Interest Group, please, read also the CIRCABC Interest Group Leaders Guide.

The CIRCABC Online Help is currently offered in English. It will be translated to the other official languages of the European Union, in the same time as the CIRCABC user interface.
