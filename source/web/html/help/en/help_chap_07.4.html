Visit the Library with the Full-edit role
 

You will find in this page all what you need to know in order to visit the Library with the Full-edit role.


You can navigate to any part of this page through the links below:

  • Add or create content
  • Edit content
  • Delete content

This is the typical role an author may be assigned. What can you do with a full-edit role? You can:

  • create contents;
  • modify your own contents;
  • modify the contents of other members;
  • delete only your own contents.
