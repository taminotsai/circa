Coming to CIRCABC
 

This help page shows how you can benefit from registering. It explains how to get a free-of-charge CIRCABC account.


You can navigate to any part of this page through the links below:

  • Requirement
  • About the access system
  • First-time user: should I register?

Requirement

CIRCABC is a web-based application, so, obviously, you need an internet connection. You also need a browser (Internet Explorer 5 or higher, Firefox…).

You must use a valid and unique e-mail address.

And you should know at least the URL of your CIRCABC application or the URL of the interest group you wish to consult or participate in.


About the access system

Users are spread into two domains according to the way they access CIRCABC:

The Commission's civil servants and external staff working "intra muros" are not requested for any initial registration to CIRCABC. They directly log in CIRCABC and apply for membership in Interest Groups (IG). Moreover, they benefit of the single sign on. That allows them to authenticate only once through the European Commission's Authentication Service (ECAS).

Other users access through the CIRCABC's authentication system.

First-time user: should I register?


Why both "Register" and "Login"?

Users, external as well as European institutions' staff, log in CIRCABC through the European Commission's Authentication Service (ECAS). You need an ECAS username and password. If you ever need several usernames, keep in mind that you must use one e-mail address per username.

You make a Login

  • to enter IGs as Member,
  • to apply for new memberships.

Register or login?

Is the access to CIRCABC public?

Although working in a private workspace, certain interest groups enable public access to some of their services. So they make some documents accessible to everybody, whether or not authenticated while the rest of their library is reserved for authenticated users.

You get in CIRCABC...

Via public access...

You are connected as a guest. No login is required but it should be noted that not all the Interest Groups have documents available this way: you can access only the contents which have been granted "public" accessibility.

  • You can only see the interest groups visible to not-logged users
  • You can only read and download documents from the interest groups open to public access

If wishing to collaborate more closely in an Interest Group, you are warmly advised to register.

Via authenticated access...

Before joining an interest group, you may be granted very limited access rights by the Interest Group Leader.
The registered users, however, may submit applications for membership in an Interest Group.

Getting a CIRCABC account


A unique username and password allows you to apply for membership of several interest groups.

Register procedure

You will obtain your own CIRCABC account by following the "Register" procedure.
You click register from the CIRCABC main menu.

This takes you to a registration form which consists of the following areas:

  • Identity (personal data and information required to let you be identified as a CIRCABC user);
  • Contact information (this may be useful for getting in touch with the other members of the IGs you will participate in);
  • Security check (information needed for checking purpose).

Register form: All of the fields marked with symbol "star" (*) are mandatory

You create your own password and user name:Your password should contain 8 characters or more. It must contain at least three types of characters in the following four :

  • upper case (A to Z);
  • lower case (a to z);
  • numeric (0 to 9);
  • special characters (namely, !"#$%&'()*+?-./:;<=>?@[\]^_`{|}~).
Why a security check?

Bulk-registration finder

This CAPCHA test verifies that the current registration has not been issued by a robot programmed for running bulk registrations. An equivalent audio message is played for visually-impaired users.

Some more about visibility of your personal information

If you wish to be easily contacted by the other members of the Interest Group and let them know more about your domain(s) of excellence, for instance, you will choose to authorize everybody in the IG to see your user information.

If not, you will deny such authorization. Only the Interest Group leader, category administrator and CIRCABC-IPM administrator will be able to access your whole user information. All of the IG other members will see only your user name, first name and surname.

Register

If you have filled out all the mandatory fields, if your password complies with the password rules and if… you are not a robot, you have just to confirm your request for an account by pressing on the "Register" button.

Your request for registration has been recorded and processed. The message below is displayed:


Congratulations!

If you ever get an error message (password does not match, e-mail missing, etc)… just correct and validate again through the "Register" button.

How and when do I receive my CIRCABC account?

Once you have filled out and submitted the registration form, you receive an e-mail with a hyperlink towards CIRCABC:


Click on this link to activate your CIRCABC account
How to activate my account?

You follow the above-mentioned hyperlink. This is an activation URL which takes you to a "Login" page. You type in the user name sent to you and the password you had created as registering. Your account is activated.
If not yet activated after 60 days, your CIRCABC account will be removed.

What if I have forgotten my user-name?

You can reset it by yourself, very easily. You follow the "forget your password?" link that can be seen on the Login page. This takes you to a "re-send password" page whereon you are requested for:

  • entering your user name
  • entering either the CAPCHA code (checking code) which is shown in the "Security check" section or its equivalent audio message:

Key in your username and the code to reset your password

You press on the "Reset password" button, located at the bottom of the page. A new password will be e-mailed to you. For a security purpose, this e-mail will not contain your user name.
As an alternative, you can contact your IG Leader.

What if I have forgotten my password?

You log in CIRCABC and select "Manage your account" from the CIRCABC main menu so as to be taken to "Edit your CIRCABC account"


Click here to access "change password"
How to change my password?

Once there, you click the link "Change your password" that can be found at the bottom of the "My Details" section.


How to change password

The "Change password" form is displayed. You are expected to put and confirm a new password. Once done, you press on button "Change" to save your new password.
