What is "Newsgroups"?

"Newsgroups" is one of the services of your Interest Group. It contains forums about any theme having raised the interest of your community of users.

Top of the page 

Who can participate in newsgroups?

Participating and performing actions in "Newsgroups" is conditioned by the role you are permitted to play in this service. Such role is assigned to you by any Interest Group administrator (your IG Leader, for instance).

Specific roles are attached to the Newsgroups service:

No access: "Newsgroups" is not listed on your main menu;

Access: You can only read posts;

Post: you read and post messages. You edit or delete your own posts;

Moderate: Further to creating topics and posts, you approve or reject any message submitted to moderation.

Administrate: you create, administer and delete forums. Consequently, you can create topics and post messages. You can also delete any topic or message.

"Newsgroups" is accessible from the interest group main menu:



CIRCABC main menu: select "Newsgroups"

What does the Newsgroups service looks like?

The newsgroups service is top-down structured:

Forums > Topics > Posts

- A Forum is a set of topics. It may also contain other forums. It may be subject to moderation.
- A Topic is a thread of discussion.
- A Post is an original message or an answer.
